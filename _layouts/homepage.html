What is a Coin Show?

A coin show is an event where dealers, collectors, and enthusiasts gather to buy, sell, and trade coins, currency, bullion, and other numismatic collectibles. Shows range from small local events to major national conventions with hundreds of dealers.

  • Dealers selling US and world coins, currency, gold and silver bullion
  • Free appraisals from experienced dealers
  • Education talks, presentations, and youth programs
  • Networking with collectors and local coin clubs

Tips for First-Time Attendees

  • Bring cash — most dealers prefer it and offer discounts
  • Arrive early for the best deals, especially at smaller shows
  • Bring a loupe or magnifying glass to examine coins
  • Set a budget before you go — it's easy to overspend
  • Ask questions — dealers are happy to educate newcomers
  • Always verify dates before traveling — shows can change
