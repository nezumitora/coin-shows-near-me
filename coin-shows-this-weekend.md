## Why Attend a Coin Show This Weekend?

Weekend coin shows are the perfect opportunity to:

- **Buy coins at dealer prices** — often below retail
- **Sell or get appraisals** on coins you already own
- **Discover rare finds** from dozens of dealers under one roof
- **Learn from experienced collectors** and numismatic experts
- **Support your local coin collecting community**

Most coin shows are free to attend and welcome everyone from first-time visitors to experienced collectors.
