## Appraisal and Valuation Disclaimer

Offers, appraisals, and valuations provided by Dealers through the Platform are:

- **Preliminary and non-binding** — final pricing is determined in person
- **Subjective opinions** of individual Dealers — not statements of fact
- **Based on limited information** — photographs and descriptions may not capture all details affecting value
- **Subject to change** upon physical inspection of items

We strongly recommend:

1. **Getting multiple opinions** — submit to multiple Dealers or attend a coin show to compare offers
2. **Not relying solely on online appraisals** for high-value items
3. **Having valuable items professionally graded** by PCGS, NGC, or other recognized grading services before selling
4. **Researching current market values** independently before accepting any offer

## Melt Value Calculator Disclaimer

The melt value calculator provided on the Platform:

- Uses spot prices entered by the user — we do not provide real-time pricing
- Calculates approximate melt value based on standard coin specifications
- Does **not** account for numismatic (collector) premiums, rarity, condition, or errors
- Should **not** be used as the sole basis for any financial decision

Actual coin values may differ significantly from melt value. Consult a qualified numismatic professional for accurate valuations.
